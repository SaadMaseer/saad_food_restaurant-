The Saad Food History

More than 10+ years of experience

At Saad Food Restaurant, we began with a simple dream to bring the rich and diverse flavors of specific cuisine or global influences to the heart of Sweden. Founded in year 2010 , our journey started with a passion for great food, quality ingredients, and a love for creating memorable dining experiences.


From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a beloved local spot where friends, families, and food lovers come together to enjoy our signature dishes, each crafted with care and tradition. Over the years, we’ve stayed true to our roots while also embracing innovation, continually evolving our menu to reflect both classic favorites and exciting new creations.
